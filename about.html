About Garyen™

We are the BIGGEST online real estate database company and rental marketplace on the web.

About Us

Garyen is a Nigerian leading online real estate database company and rental marketplace dedicated to empowering consumers with required data and knowledge around the place they call their home, connecting them with the best local professionals who can provide them with the much-needed assistance through recommendations, local insights and map overlay that offer details on commute reports, crime, schools, churches and nearby businesses.

Garyen serves a full lifecycle of owning and living in a home: buying, selling, renting, financing and remodelling. Garyen’s living database include homes for sale, homes for rent and estates current in development as well as estimates of home value, rent estimates and home-related information.

Garyen charges property managers and management companies to advertise their listings on the Garyen platform which includes the web and mobile app. Garyen sends qualified leads – prospective renters and buyers - to advertisers to help them maximize the return on investment of their funds. We also facilitate our visitors to get rent & purchases estimates (G-estimates) of properties, and also enable them to find properties and detailed information about these properties.

Garyen provides price trends information by using listing and public data which shows how the price of a home has changed over a period of time as well as comparing that house price with other homes in the same zip code, city or state.

Leveraging on cutting-edge technology, Garyen spans across major cities in West Africa and has been able to build a strong network in real estate through intelligent systems development and information technology.

Garyen also works with developing nations and partners with leading technology companies to pioneer radical innovations in business, healthcare and agriculture in order to strengthen indigenous economies.
