Happy Hours

Garyen™ believes people who party together, work better together. Each month, a team hosts a themed happy hour for all employees, complete with tasty bites, soda, cocktails, and entertainment.

We Nurture Innovation

Innovation Week.

Our culture emphasizes autonomy and innovation; therefore, we host a quarterly “Innovation Week” as dedicated time for all employees and interns to experiment with new product ideas or passion projects. We like to think of Innovation Week as a weeklong hackathon that the whole company can participate in-pitch an idea (no matter how wild), gather a team and build it. We encourage employees to work on big projects with new technologies and teammates.
